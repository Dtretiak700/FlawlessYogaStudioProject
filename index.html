About our studio

The studio is located in the center of city. Light space, attention to detail and our professional team set you up for quality practice.
Before and after the class, we invite you to ask questions to teachers and administrators or share your impressions with other guests over a cup of aromatic herbal tea.

For your convenience, the studio provides personal lockers, showers, towels, hair dryers, as well as ethical body and hair products.
